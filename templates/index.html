Burn After Reading

Put some secret information in below. Or even some boring non-secret information. The secret will be encrypted and stored.

You will receive a link that allows you to retrieve the secret later. Send that link to whomever you want to share the secret with. As soon as somebody opens the link and retrieves the secret, the stored secret will be deleted.

Secrets will expire 24 hours after they were created.

Note: The secrets are encrypted in the database using AES-256 with symmetric GPG using the shared key you get in your URL. I don't store or log that shared key, but you should decide for yourself whether you trust me before using this for secrets you care about.
